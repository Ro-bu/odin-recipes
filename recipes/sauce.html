THE SAUCE

Description

The sauce mr ramsay makes is out of t his world. Decadent taste, rich and creamy texture and an aroma that is hard to describe

The first time mr ramsay made it, he was just a child. At 7 years old in the grimey streets of Worcheshire England with only three ingredients - a tomato, fresh milk, and some basil. Over the years, as his palette has developed, he has added more ingredients but the base of the sauce remains the same.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Milk
  • Basil
  • salt
  • LEttuce

Steps

  1. Peel the tomato
  2. Heat the milk to 70 degrees
  3. Chop the lettuce and basil
  4. Mix all together over high heat
